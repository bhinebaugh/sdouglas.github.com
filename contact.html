Graduate students: email to schedule a rotation

Postdocs: send CV and references, and responses to the following questions:

  1. What aspect of the research in the lab interests you most? How might it connect to your graduate research strengths?
  2. What are your plans for obtaining postdoctoral fellowship funding?
  3. Are you legally authorized to work in the United States? Will you require sponsorship for employment visa status?
  4. What are your career goals?

Berkeley undergrads: preference given to anyone who completes H7B and (CS61B or E7).

Other undergrads: Join one of the graduate programs at UCSF. Bonus points if you start or join a BIOMOD team beforehand.
