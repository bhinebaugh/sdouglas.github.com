Research

What do we do?

A short video introduction to our research can be found here. We aim to use the same building blocks as life to create novel tools and devices that perform useful tasks on the nanometer scale. Our publications page provides recent examples of our work.

How do we do it?

Most of our innovation is focused on biomolecular design, method development, and writing software to support these efforts.
