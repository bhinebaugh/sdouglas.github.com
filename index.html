What do we do?

We aim to use the same building blocks as life to create novel tools and devices that perform useful tasks on the nanometer scale. A short video introduction to our research can be found here.

How do we do it?

We build with biomolecules, so we can rely on well-established techniques from molecular biology such as cloning, gel electrophoresis, and electron microscopy. We also write a lot of software to facilitate our design process.
